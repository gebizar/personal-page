About Me

Hey There!

As a recent graduate of the University of Washington Information School, I strive to leverage my broad skillset to shape the ever-changing landscape of the tech-world. Having been raised in Singapore, Taiwan, and the U.S., my diverse upbringing has strengthened my need to be inclusionary, and therefore bolster diversity in any community I belong to. My want to bridge communities is further enforced, by my two-years as an resident adviser in which I utilized Mandarin to engage with International Students and eventually incorporate them as part of the larger UW community. In addition to being in student leadership, having participated in many project teams through school and my internship in Intel has given me insight to aligning project visions between those of engineers, project managers, and data scientists. My experience in having spent hours tirelessly coding and enthusiastically engaging with those that I've presented projects to (regardless of language) has culminated in my ability to bring visions to life on all fronts.
